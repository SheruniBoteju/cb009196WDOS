Donations

Monetary and or Equipmejt Donations will be highly appreciated to help us continue the care and well being of these majestic animals. This will aid us in many ways which will help us strive to our goal to provide premium security, love and care to the animals living with us.

You can Donate by either online transferring your donation to our official account or writing a checque with amount and mailing it to us or even handing it over to the main office in the premises. You can also donate various equipment to the Zoo as well. Heavy duty hoses, maintenance equipment are greatly appreciated. You can refer the main Office for further information on Equipment Donations.
